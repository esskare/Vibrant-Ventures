Our Activities

Camp and Meditate

Camping and meditating together create a harmonious blend, fostering a deep connection with oneself and the awe-inspiring beauty of the outdoors, resulting in a truly enriching experience for the mind, body, and soul.

Zip Lining

The adrenaline rush and the breathtaking views make zip lining a favorite activity for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. It is a fantastic way to experience the thrill of flight, conquer fears, and create unforgettable memories.

Hiking

It involves traversing various terrains, such as mountains, forests, or trails, on foot. Hiking offers a unique opportunity to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with the natural world.

Game drives

Game drives offer an exhilarating and immersive experience in the heart of nature's wilderness. They take participants on a thrilling journey through vast landscapes, allowing them to encounter diverse wildlife in their natural habitats and from the comfort of specially designed vehicles.

Rafting

Rafting allows participants to immerse themselves in the power of nature as they paddle through turbulent waters, maneuver around rocks. It involves navigating through fast-flowing rivers using an inflatable raft which is a thrilling adventure that combines teamwork, adrenaline, and natural beauty.

Sky Diving

As thrill-seekers leap from an aircraft high above the ground, they are instantly immersed in a rush of excitement and a sense of freedom like no other. The sheer thrill of freefalling through the sky, surrounded by breathtaking views, creates an unparalleled feeling of exhilaration and awe.
